User Attributes for ABAC

These attributes are included in JWT tokens and sent to OPA for policy evaluation

Role

Defines the user's permission level: admin (full access), manager (department oversight), or employee (basic access).

Department

Used for department-based access control: engineering, HR, finance, marketing, sales, operations, management, or general.

Designation

Job title that enables fine-grained access decisions, such as "Software Engineer" or "Senior Manager".
